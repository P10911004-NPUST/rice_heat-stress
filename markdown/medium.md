# Half-MS medium preparation

| Chemicals               | Concentration  | Addition             | Location       | Final concentration |
| :--------               | :---:          | :---:                | :---:          | :---:               |
| MS basal salt           | &mdash;        | 8.66 g               | &#8545;        | 8.66 g / L          |
| MES hydrate             | &mdash;        | 2 g                  | &#8545;        | 2 g / L             |
| PPM&trade; (optional)   | 100%           | 500 &micro;L         | 4&deg;C ( a )  | 0.05%               |
| KOH <sup>&#8251;</sup>  | 5 M            | ~ 700 &micro;L       | &#8550;        | &mdash;             |
| ddH<sub>2</sub>O        | &mdash;        | 1 L                  |                |                     |

The final pH should be 5.7 ~ 5.8.

<sup>&#8251;</sup> KOH is used for pH adjustment. To prepare 5 M KOH solution, dilute 330 g of 85% KOH in 1 L ddH<sub>2</sub>O.
Since KOH will release heat during dissolution, it is important to add the KOH granules into the water gradually and slowly.

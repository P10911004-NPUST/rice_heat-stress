Polymerase Chain Reaction ( PCR )

Preparation

Material ※ Concentration Addition per sample Location
gDNA † 10 ng / uL 4 µL Usually -20°C
F-primer 0.4 µL -20°C (2row-3col-4layer)
R-primer 0.4 µL -20°C (2row-3col-4layer)
2X-Taq 5 µL -20°C (4row-3col-4layer; orange lid)
Ambion water 0.2 µL RNA bench (right-hand side, top drawer)
Total : 10 µL

※ All materials should be placed on ice.

† gDNA : The gDNA stock solution of all samples should be standardized to
the same concentration, for example 10 ng/µL.


Procedure

  1. Prepare the total amount of the required working solution ( F-primer, R-primer, 2X-Taq );
  • For example, if we had 10 samples, then me make the working solution by adding
    4 µL F-primer,
    4 µL R-primer,
    and 50 µL 2X-Taq into a new 200 µL PCR tube.

  1. Dispense the working solution into a new 200 µL PCR tubes ( 5.8 µL for each sample );

  2. Lastly, add the sample gDNA stock solution;

  3. Close the lid tightly, and swipe the tube gently with finger several time to mix the solution well;

  4. Slightly centrifuge to bring down the solution;

  5. Put into the PCR machine ( the machine should be warmed-up around 2 mins before use ).

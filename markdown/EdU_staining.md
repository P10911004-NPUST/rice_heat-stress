# Experimental Procedure

## Step 1: EdU incubation &rarr; labeling cells with EdU
1. Incubate the roots with the <b style='color: violet'>diluted Component A</b> ( 10&micro;M ).
- For example, each falcon tube contain 15 mL diluted Component A, and directly immersed the roots in the solution, DO NOT cut the shoot part, the plant should be still alive;
2. Incubate under <b style='color: violet'>room temperature</b> for <b style='color: violet'>2~4 hours</b>;

## Step 2: Fixation
1. Remove the incubation media;
2. Add 100&micro;L of <b style='color: violet'>fixative solution</b> ( 3.7 ~ 4% formaldehyde in PBS );
3. Incubate for <b style='color: violet'>15 minutes</b> at <b style='color: violet'>room temperature</b>;
4. Remove fixative solution and <b style='color: violet'>wash twice with PBS</b>.

- <b style='color: orange'>You can safely store the samples at this time within the wash solution.</b>

## Step 3: Permeabilization
1. Remove the wash solution;
2. Add 100&micro;L of <b style='color: violet'>permeabilization reagent</b>;
3. Incubate for <b style='color: violet'>15 minutes</b> at <b style='color: violet'>room temperature</b>.

## Step 4: EdU detection
1. Dilute the 10X <b style='color: violet'>Component E</b> to 1X with ddH<sub>2</sub>O;  
- Prepare only as much of this solution as necessary for that day's experiments, and use on the same day.

2. Add the <b style='color: violet'>Click-iT&reg; reaction cocktail</b> as follow. 
- Add the incredients in the order listed in the table; otherwise, the reaction will not proceed optimally. 
- Use the cocktail immediately after preparation. The Click-iT&reg; reaction buffer additive is susceptable to oxidation and is the limiting factor to the Click-iT&reg; reaction cocktail's effectiveness over time.

|  Component  |  Material name                         |  Addition (&micro;L)  |
|  :---:      |  :---                                  |  :---:                |
|  C          |  Click-iT&reg; EdU reaction buffer     |  850                  |
|  D          |  CuSO<sub>4</sub>                      |  40                   |
|  B          |  Alexa Fluor&reg; azide                |  2.5                  |
|  E          |  Click-iT&reg; EdU buffer additive     |  100                  |
|  Total:     |                                        |  ~ 1000 &micro;L      |
||||

3. Remove the permeabilization reagent;
4. Wash twice with PBS;
5. Remove wash solution and add <b style='color: violet'>100 &micro;L</b> of <b style='color: violet'>Click-iT&reg; reaction cocktail</b>;
6. Incubate for <b style='color: violet'>30 minutes</b> at <b style='color: violet'>room temperature</b>. Must be <b style='color: violet'>protected from light</b>;
7. Remove the reaction cocktail and wash once with 100 &micro;L of Click-iT&reg; reaction rince buffer ( <b style='color: violet'>Component F</b> );
8. Proceed to confocal imaging and analysis.

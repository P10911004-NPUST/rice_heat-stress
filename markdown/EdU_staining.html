  • "ddH2O" means deionized water;
  • "complete medium" means the medium used to cultivate the samples. for example, half-MS medium.

Experimental Procedure

Step 1: Labeling

  1. Prepare incubation medium containing 10 µM Component A;
  • For example, in 5 samples scenario, add 100 µL Component A ( 10 mM ) into 100 mL half-MS medium for the experiment. Then, repackage the diluted Component A to new falcon tubes, each tube takes around 20 mL for each sample ( make sure the root tips can be fully submerged into the solution).
  1. Incubate the plants in the incubation medium in the same cultivation condition for 1 hour.
  • Directly immersed the roots in the solution, DO NOT cut the shoot part, the plant should still alive. The root should be fully submerged into the solution. Do not over-pushing the root to avoid roots injury.

Step 2: Fixation

  1. Cut the root tip and immerse in 200 µL fixative solution in eppendorf;
  2. Incubate for 30 minutes under room temperature;
  3. Remove fixative solution and add 200 µL of 1X PBS to wash the roots. Repeat this wash procedure three times, for each 10 mins ( 3 × 10 mins ).
  • You can safely store the samples in 1X PBS solution up to 24 hours after washed out the fixative solution.

Step 3: Detection

Prepare the cocktail

  1. Firstly, dilute the Component C and Component E from 10X to 1X using ddH2O. Calculate the total usage amount according to the following tables.
  • Prepare the Component C and E as much as necessary only for that day's experiments, and use on the same day.
  1. Add the Click-iT® reaction cocktail ingredients as follow.
  • Add the incredients in the order listed in the table; otherwise, the reaction will not proceed optimally.
  • Use the cocktail immediately after preparation. The Click-iT® reaction buffer additive is susceptable to oxidation and is the limiting factor to the Click-iT® reaction cocktail's effectiveness over time.
Component Material name Addition per sample Add for 4 samples
C 1X Click-iT® EdU reaction buffer 85 µL 340 µL
D CuSO4 4 µL 16 µL
B Alexa Fluor® azide 0.25 µL 1 µL
E 1X Click-iT® EdU buffer additive 10 µL 40 µL
Total: ~ 100 µL ~ 400 µL

Incubation

  1. Remove wash solution and add 100 µL of Click-iT® reaction cocktail for each sample;
  2. Incubate for 30 minutes at room temperature. Must be protected from light;
  3. Remove the reaction cocktail and wash once with 100 µL of Click-iT® reaction rince buffer ( Component F );
  4. Wash 3 times with 1X PBS ( 3 × 10 mins );
  5. Mounted into Fluoromount-G anti-fade solution. Proceed to confocal imaging and analysis.

  1. Use ImageJ with bioformats_package.jar plugin to proceed the confocal images;
  • The plugin ( bioformats_package.jar ) can be downloaded from https://www.openmicroscopy.org/bio-formats/downloads/
  • The plugin should be placed in the "/ImageJ/plugins/jars" directory

  1. Open the confocal .czi file with the ImageJ, the "Bio-Formats Import Options" will automatically pop up;

  2. Select the options as follow:

  1. Select the region of interest (ROI) and measure.
